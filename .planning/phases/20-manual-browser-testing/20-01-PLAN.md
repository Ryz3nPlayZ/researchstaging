    - Verify sidebar collapses to icons-only

    - Resize browser to 375x667 (iPhone SE)
    - Verify hamburger menu appears in top-left
    - Click hamburger and verify drawer slides in from left
    - Verify AI chat is hidden on mobile
    - Verify content stacks vertically
    - Verify no horizontal scroll
    - Test all main views on mobile: Dashboard, Files, Library, Editor, Analysis, Memory

    For each viewport, record: PASS or FAIL with specific issues.
